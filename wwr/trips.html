Reservations can be made by clicking on our "Book Now" button or via phone by calling 613-330-2839. Reservations made online require at least 3 days advanced notice by filling the online form. Some dates are unavailable if fully booked. Contact us for more information.
Time Activity Description
Morning Meet and Greet Meet at the designated location and sign waivers. Receve a safety briefing and instructions from our team guide
- - - - - Gear Up and Paddle Training Get outfitted with wetsuits, helmets, and life jackets. Practice paddling techniques
Midday Whitewater Rafting Adventure begin Embark on the rafting journey, navigating through various rapids with your guide. Enjoy scenic view.
Lunch BBQ Lunch on the river Stop on a beach or island for BBQ lunch (included in your booking)
Afternoon More Whitewater Rafting Continue navigating the river, tackling more challenging rapids.
Late Afternoon Return Debriefing Arrive back at the base, return gear, and participate in a debriefing session with our team.
